How Do I Contact MacPaw Customer Support Number?

If you're a user of MacPaw products like CleanMyMac X, Setapp, or Gemini, you might be looking for a way to reach customer support. Is There a MacPaw Customer Support Phone Number?

As of now, MacPaw does not offer a public customer service phone number. The company operates entirely through online channels. While this may seem limiting, MacPaw has developed a strong digital support system that provides fast, effective assistance via live chat, email, and a dedicated Help Center.

How to Contact MacPaw Support

Here are the best ways to reach MacPaw’s support team:

  • Help Center: Visit macpawcleanmypc.support for FAQs, troubleshooting, and guides.
  • Live Chat: Available via the Zendesk widget on MacPaw’s website.
  • Email Support: Submit a ticket through their support form or by replying to previous emails from MacPaw.

When to Contact Support

Typical reasons to reach out to MacPaw include:

  • Problems activating or installing software
  • Billing errors or refund requests
  • Subscription questions (renewals, cancellations)
  • Crashes or performance issues
  • Lost license key or account access

What You Should Provide

For faster resolution, include the following in your support request:

  • Your MacPaw account email
  • Order number or license key
  • Description of the issue and steps to reproduce
  • Screenshots or logs if applicable
  • Your macOS and product version

How Fast Is MacPaw Support?

MacPaw typically responds within 24 to 48 hours, though users often report quicker replies—especially through live chat. Response time may vary based on volume, but their team is known for being professional, knowledgeable, and helpful.

Self-Service Alternatives

If you want to try resolving your issue before contacting support, MacPaw’s Help Center is a great resource. It includes:

  • Guides for CleanMyMac, Setapp, Gemini, and more
  • Instructions for managing subscriptions
  • Troubleshooting tips for app crashes or bugs
  • Uninstall steps and account recovery options

Why MacPaw Doesn't Use Phone Support

MacPaw focuses on providing efficient digital support. By handling all customer inquiries online, they can route issues to the right teams quickly, offer step-by-step solutions, and maintain a detailed knowledge base. This approach often leads to faster and more effective results than phone support could offer.
